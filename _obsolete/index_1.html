Brief bio
I received my Bachelor degree in Information Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University(XJTU) in 2003. I had my Master degree in Computer Science from Nanyang Technological University(NTU) in 2011.
Besides, I worked for SAMSUNG Electronics during 2003 and 2004. I also obtained a Master degree of Control Science from Shandong University(SDU) in 2008. In 2006, I won the Oversea Exchange Scholarship from SDU, and visited school of EEE, NTU from 2007 to 2008.  Moreover, I worked for Centre for Signal Processing(NTU), Game Lab(NTU) and Video Image Processing Lab(NUS) when I was in Singapore.
